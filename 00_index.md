## What this project is

A self-directed data science learning journey, structured as a real research project exploring how humans across cultures describe consciousness, meaning, and the self. Full details in [02_project_brief.md](02_project_brief.md).

**Working sub-question:** #2 — how the language of consciousness shifted from religious to neuroscientific over the last 200 years.

**Repo:** https://github.com/zzeeshann/data-science-journey
